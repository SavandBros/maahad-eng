About Us

Al Maahad Engineering established in 2006 in Sharjah, UAE, is an innovative organization which is specialist in machinery heavy-duty attachments such as mechanical and rotating or non-rotating hydraulic rock grapples, clamshells, buckets, drum cutters, long reach and etc.

It offers extensive and various types of heavy and light attachments manufactured based on engineering principles and technical standards for domestic and foreign customers.
